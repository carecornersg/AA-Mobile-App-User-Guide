## Stage 2: Accept the Case

### Step 1: Open the Notification

Tap the push notification to open the app and view full case details.

### Step 2: Review Case Details

Check all the information:

- Interview time and date
- Location (use the map to check distance)
- Youth details
- Any special requirements

### Step 3: Accept or Decline

- ‚úÖ **Accept:** Tap **Accept** if you are available. For B1, you will be added to the selection pool. For B2/B3, first to accept is moved to confirmation immediately.
- ‚ùå **Decline (no button):** There is **no Decline button** on this broadcast notification. If you cannot support the case, simply **close the notification** (treated as decline/unavailable) and the case will continue broadcasting to other AAs (no penalty).

## Stage 3: Selection Pool (B1 Only)

### What Happens After Accepting

For **B1 broadcasts**, after you tap Accept:

- You are added to the **Selection Pool**
- The system enters a **Holding Period (5 minutes)**
- All AAs who accept during this period are collected
- After 5 minutes, the system uses **weighted selection** to choose one AA

### üîÑ Withdraw Selection Button

**While waiting in the Selection Pool:**

- You will see a **Withdraw Selection** button
- Tap this button if you change your mind
- Withdrawing removes you from the selection pool
- **No penalty** for withdrawing during holding period

- **No holding period** ‚Äì First Come First Serve (FCFS)
- First AA to tap Accept is immediately moved to Confirmation stage
- No Withdraw Selection button (instant assignment)

## Stage 4: Confirm Assignment

### When You Receive Confirmation Notification

If you are **selected** from the pool (B1) or **first to accept** (B2/B3), you will receive an **Assignment Confirmation Notification**.

### Review Full Case Details

Open the notification and review complete case information:

- **Youth Details** ‚Äì Name, age, and relevant information
- **IO Contact** ‚Äì Name and phone number
- **Location** ‚Äì Full address with interactive map
- **Interview Time** ‚Äì Scheduled date and time
- **Special Requirements** ‚Äì Any specific case requirements

### Make Your Decision

#### ‚úÖ Confirm

- Tap **Confirm** to accept the assignment
- The case is now officially assigned to you
- You will receive an interview reminder 2 hours before

#### ‚ùå Decline

- Tap **Decline** to reject the assignment
- The case will be re-broadcast to other AAs
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Warning:** This results in a **Low Priority flag for 14 days**

## Low Priority Flag

‚ö†Ô∏è **Warning about declining at confirmation stage:**

If you decline or timeout without response after being selected:

- You receive a **Low Priority flag for 14 days**
- Lower chance of being selected in weighted selection (B1)
- This ensures commitment to accepted cases

üìå **Note:** Withdrawing during B1 holding period does **NOT** result in Low Priority flag.

## After Confirmation

Once you confirm the assignment:

1. **Interview Reminder:** You will receive a reminder **2 hours before** the scheduled interview
2. **View Details:** Go to **My Assignments** to view case details anytime
3. **Contact IO:** You can contact the IO directly using the provided phone number

## Troubleshooting

**Didn't receive broadcast notification:**

- Check phone notification settings are enabled
- Check if you're in Quiet Hours or pause mode
- Verify your region and timing preferences match

**Didn't receive confirmation notification:**

- Check your phone's notification settings
- Check if you're in Quiet Hours mode
- You may not have been selected (B1)
